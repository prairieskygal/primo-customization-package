SAIS Europe Library Search

Search Catalyst for books, articles, journals, digital media, and more within the Library's collection plus subscription databases and journal collections.

Catalyst also helps JHU students manage their research. Sign in with your JHED to:

  • Renew checked out materials
  • Create lists of bookmarked items
  • Export citations to popular citation managers
  • View full search results (some databases only show results when you're signed in)

Inter-Library Loan

Inter-library loan is available only to libraries and not individuals. Some materials (reference items, periodicals, etc.) are excluded from lending, and a fee is charged for each loan. For information email EvansLibrary@jhu.edu.
